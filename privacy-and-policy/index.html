Privacy & Policy

Cyber Slot Store is an open-source software catalog. This page is written to be read, not skimmed past — here's exactly what we collect (nothing), what stays on your device, and how the catalog works.

 No login required  No payment requests  No advertisements  No telemetry
01 / INFORMATION WE COLLECT
We don't collect personal information

Cyber Slot Store does not collect any personal information. Full stop. Here's the explicit list of things that never touch our servers:

Names
Email addresses
Passwords
Phone numbers
Card details
Banking info
Government IDs
Personal files
Device identifiers

02 / STORED LOCALLY
Preferences stay in your browser

To improve your experience, a few interface preferences are saved only inside your own browser and are never transmitted to our servers.

Theme Accent color Compact layout Grid density UI preferences
03 / DOWNLOADS
Nothing is hosted on our servers

Cyber Slot Store does not host software downloads itself. Every download routes through the original source:

Official GitHub repositoriesverified
Official GitHub releasesverified
Other trusted developer pageswhen listed

Source code is available for review whenever possible.

04 / COOKIES
No tracking cookies, ever

Cyber Slot Store does not use tracking cookies. Your browser may store local preferences for interface customization, but these never leave your device.
